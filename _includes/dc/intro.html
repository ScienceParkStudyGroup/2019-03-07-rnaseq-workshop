This workshop will give a first introduction to a typical messenger RNA-Seq analysis by combining a short lecture with an extensive practical hands-on session.
Upon completion of this workshop, you'll be able to:

  • understand the main steps of a typical mRNA-Seq analysis.
  • know some basic information about how (Illumina) sequencing works.
  • obtain some hands-on experience on remote/cloud computing
  • know what NGS quality checks actually mean.
  • know more about read trimming
  • perform read mapping
  • count mapped reads and normalise these counts
  • perform differential expression and some other post-alignment steps
Participants will be encouraged to help one another and to apply what they have learned to their own research problems.
